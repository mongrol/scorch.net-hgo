>moolah v0.6 - Thoughts on releases

  • December 29, 2008
  • The Skreegs  
  • moolah, Tech  

>A few weeks ago I knocked out the first release of moolah. This, being 0.5 and pretty rough, was done under the “release early, release often” advice that google and many other Open Source developers preach. Does this really hold for a Mac app though? In general, Mac users aren’t used to unfinished, unpolished, buggy apps. They’re a bit “ordinary” (I suppose you could almost say snobby) in that they like they’re apps to be v1.0 at least, nicely polished, and without bugs.
